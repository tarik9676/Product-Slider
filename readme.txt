Product Slider simply help you make a slideshow for your chosen products or the latest products.

== Description ==

Product Slider is a free plugin. As it says it's slideshow plugin for your ecommerce products. It absolutely has desktop, tablet & mobile responsiveness. It's beautiful design shines your products and makes them atrractive.

It's several settings in WordPress admin menu. Such switching auto slider, interval time, slider number, custom products and many more.

Major features in Product Slider:

*   Sliders can be either latest or custom (by ID's)
*   Integrated with cool animation.
*   User can change image width
*   User can hide and shows Arrows & Dots
*   Shortcode for the slider is [show_devtarik_product_slider]
